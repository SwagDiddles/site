Values

 I value timely delivery, accuracy, and reliability. As a Developer, it goes without saying that the user wants the product at their fingertips as soon as possible. In order to meet deadlines, I keep clear track of my time and how to jump from one task to another as well as the order to accomplish tasks. This leads into accuracy. In order to be accurate, it is important that I lay out a schedule with soft deadlines and allowing myself time to go back to catch any errors. The product is for the user and you must cater it to their needs/desires. Last, reliability. More than anything, I wish to serve as a reliable developer who exceeds expectations, accepts and is open to criticism, and is constantly looking for more avenues to grow. Not only am I to be held reliable, but the product I will be providing to users as well.
